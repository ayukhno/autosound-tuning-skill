Hey everyone! 👋

While dialing in my car for competition, the AI I tune with proposed a new target curve and ended up building a little curve visualizer. I added a few things, and it grew into a genuinely handy free tool. Wanted to share it 🙂

The best part: right-click any point on the chart and it shows how a boost or cut at that frequency shapes the sound, and which instruments and vocal traits live in that range. It's built on the listening knowledge we gathered while creating the tuning skill.

It ships with SQ-Comp-Ref and a flat reference built in. Drop in any other curve (your own from REW, or standard ones from the Nono Tuning Tool) to compare them side by side. Fully offline, free and open-source.

👉 Try it: https://ayukhno.github.io/autosound-tuning-skill/_curve-visualizer.html
